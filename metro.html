Tired of the Commuting Chaos?

Unexpected delays, confusing interchanges, and fragmented information make commuting a daily source of stress. You deserve a smarter way to travel.

Unexpected Disruptions

Official alerts are often delayed, leaving you stranded on a crowded platform with no idea what to do next.

Confusing Stations

Navigating large interchanges like Dhoby Ghaut or finding the right exit can feel like solving a maze, especially during peak hours.

Information Overload

Juggling multiple apps for train times, disruption alerts, and route planning is inefficient and frustrating.

Introducing MetroAh: The All-in-One Solution

MetroAh integrates every tool you need into one intelligent app, providing assistance at every stage of your journey.

Proactive Disruption Alerts

Get instant, real-time notifications about delays, so you can adapt your route before you even leave.

AR Live View Navigation

Follow augmented reality directions to effortlessly find your platform, the nearest exit, or station amenities.

Intelligent Route Planning

Receive multiple route options that consider crowd levels and active disruptions, ensuring the fastest journey.

"Nearest Door" Guidance

Know exactly which train car to board to minimize walking distance during transfers, saving you precious time.

Real-Time Crowd Data

Check station and platform crowd levels before you arrive to avoid the squeeze and travel in comfort.

Multi-Stop Trip Planning

Planning a day of errands? Add multiple destinations and let MetroAh calculate the most efficient route.

Built for Every Commuter

Whether you travel daily or just occasionally, MetroAh is designed to solve your biggest commuting challenges.

For Wei Ming, the Daily Commuter

Student, travels every day

Pain Point: Being caught off-guard by disruptions, making him late for class.

Our Solution: Proactive alerts notify him of a delay on his line. The app instantly suggests an alternative route, considering crowd levels, ensuring he still gets to his exam on time.

For Mrs. Lim, the Occasional Traveler

Explorer, unfamiliar with interchanges

Pain Point: Getting lost in large, unfamiliar stations and not knowing which exit to take.

Our Solution: Upon arrival, she uses the AR Live View. Her phone's camera shows clear, overlaid directions guiding her step-by-step to the correct exit to meet her friends, stress-free.
